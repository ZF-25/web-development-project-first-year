Brain Worm

About Brain Worm

Brain Worm is a collaborative learning platform designed for students to share notes, explore academic topics, and learn together. It encourages knowledge sharing and makes studying more interactive and efficient.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a simple and accessible platform where students can improve their learning experience through collaboration and shared resources.

Key Features

  • User registration and login system
  • Create and share study posts
  • Browse topics by categories
  • Upload and view learning resources
